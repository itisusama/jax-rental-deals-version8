Common questions

Stuff people ask us all the time.

Is it free to search listings?

Completely free. No sign-up required to browse. We don't charge renters anything, ever — not for applications, not for introductions, not for anything.

How do I list my property?

Send us a message with the address, rent price, and a few photos. We'll verify the listing, write up a description, and have it live within 48 hours. Also free.

Are the listings verified?

Yes — 98% of active listings have been confirmed by our team within the last 30 days. We call landlords, check photos, and pull public records before anything goes live.

What neighborhoods do you cover?

Riverside, Avondale, San Marco, Springfield, Northbank, Downtown, Neptune Beach, Atlantic Beach, Mandarin, Murray Hill, Ortega, and Arlington — and growing.
