How we started

A rental search that felt more like a
neighbor's recommendation.

jaxrentaldeals started in 2021, when two Riverside renters got fed up with copy-paste listings, ghost properties, and out-of-state platforms that couldn't tell Avondale from Arlington. We figured Jacksonville deserved something better — a site that treats renters like adults and treats landlords like the local business owners most of them are.

We started with 11 listings and a shared Google Sheet. Word spread through Five Points coffee shops, San Marco neighborhood groups, and a whole lot of front-porch conversations. By the end of the first year we had 200+ active listings and a reputation for actually picking up the phone.

Today we cover every corner of Jacksonville — from the Northbank lofts to the beach bungalows at Atlantic and Neptune — with a team that lives here, rents here, and genuinely cares which neighborhoods are worth the drive.

What we stand for

Four things we won't
compromise on.

🔍

Every listing is real

We manually verify every property before it goes live. No stock photos of places that don't exist. No bait-and-switch pricing. If it's on jaxrentaldeals, someone on our team has confirmed it's actually available at that price.

🤝

No broker nonsense

We don't charge renters application fees or broker commissions. Listing is free for landlords too. We earn our keep by building a marketplace people trust and return to — not by nickel-and-diming both sides of every deal.

📍

Hyper-local knowledge

We can tell you which blocks in Springfield are gentrifying quickly, which Riverside streets flood after a hard rain, and which Neptune Beach landlords actually respond to maintenance requests. That kind of knowledge only comes from living here.

📞

Humans answer the phone

When you call (904) 555-0147, a real person picks up. Not a chatbot, not a voicemail labyrinth. Someone who knows Jacksonville rentals and wants to actually help you find a place you'll be happy in.
